/about

bee wertheimer (they/she) is a writer, cultural critic, and gamemaker based in nyc. they are a news reporter at kotaku and a games columnist at a.v. club, and have bylines at unwinnable and into the spine. bee's work focuses on interactive media and its potential to foster human connection within and beyond narrative.

bee holds a bachelor of fine arts from pratt institute with a major in game design and a minor in creative writing. outside of games writing, bee enjoys cooking, web design, and playing with their cat coda.
